FANS

The Friends of Arriving New Students programme helps newcomers settle in during their first weeks at Westminster.

Stats

Newcomers pair with trained students who’ve already been through it.

Moving away from home, family and friends can be daunting, but the FANS programme receives positive feedback every year, and offers volunteers the chance to earn crucial employability prospects.

What they say

Faizullah Muhammedali, BA Criminology

“I really enjoyed the FANS process and role. It was nice to give new students information and advice I never received during my own first week.

The training was absolutely brilliant and the skills I learned were helpful not only in my role, but with other jobs and roles which followed. I would strongly recommend the scheme to everyone.”

Henna Khan, BA Business Management

“I’m so happy that I signed up to FANS - it was one of the best university experiences I’ve had.

Not only do you get to help new students but you also work on yourself. Being a FAN made me more confident and able to work with new people.

It’s a shame that I’m graduating and won’t be able to do it again next year.”
